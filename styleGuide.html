Typography Usage Guidelines
  • Primary Font: Inter (Google Fonts) - Modern, clean, and highly readable
  • Font Weights: Regular (400), Medium (500), SemiBold (600), Bold (700)
  • Line Height: 1.2 for headers, 1.6 for body text
  • Letter Spacing: Slightly increased (0.02em) for buttons and uppercase text
  • Hierarchy: Use color and weight to establish clear information hierarchy

Button Usage Guidelines
  • Primary Button: Main call-to-action, uses brand orange (#FF6600)
  • Secondary Button: Alternative actions, uses dark gray (#050505)
  • Outline Button: Less prominent actions, transparent with orange border
  • Ghost Button: Subtle actions, minimal styling with hover effects
  • Accessibility: Minimum 48px height, focus states, semantic HTML
  • Hover Effects: Subtle lift animation (-2px transform) with enhanced shadows
  • Typography: Uppercase, 600 font weight, 0.02em letter spacing

Brand Guidelines

Logo Usage
  • MarineStream™ logo available in white, color, blue, and black variants
  • Maintain clear space around logo equal to the height of the "M" in MarineStream
  • Never stretch, skew, or alter the logo proportions
  • Use appropriate contrast - white logo on dark backgrounds, dark logo on light backgrounds
Color Application
  • Primary Orange (#FF6600): Call-to-action buttons, highlights, brand accents, links
  • Orange Dark (#E05A00): Hover states, emphasis, section dividers
  • Text Colors: #050505 for headers, #333333 for body text, #666666 for supporting text
  • Backgrounds: White (#FFFFFF) primary, Light Gray (#F9F9F9) secondary
Voice & Tone
  • Professional: Technical expertise with clear, authoritative communication
  • Innovative: Cutting-edge technology and forward-thinking solutions
  • Trustworthy: Reliable, compliant, and environmentally responsible
  • Accessible: Complex concepts explained in understandable terms
Design Principles
  • Clean & Modern: Minimalist design with plenty of white space
  • Consistent: Unified design system across all touchpoints
  • Accessible: WCAG compliant with proper contrast and focus states
  • Responsive: Optimized for all devices and screen sizes
  • Performance: Fast loading with optimized assets
